# Reconciliation identity

**Tracked balance change = Budget net + Sum of transfers (across all tracked accounts).**

The accounting fact that makes household reconciliation possible. Every krona that moves through tracked accounts must land in one of three buckets: income (counted in budget), expense (counted in budget), or transfer (excluded from budget). The sum of all three equals the change in total tracked balance — to the öre. When the identity holds, every krona is accounted for; when it doesn't, either categorization is wrong or balance data is misread. First applied during the April 2026 reconciliation, where it surfaced both a sort-order bug (SEB exports are reverse-chronological) and the deliberate Ulf Personal "split categorization" design that creates a −9 000/+9 000 mirror in the transfer/expense buckets.

*First encountered: 2026-05-10*
