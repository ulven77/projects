# Necromancer — Project Index

A medieval LitRPG with the science of magic grounded in Galenic medicine, Aristotelian natural philosophy, and humoral theory. The story follows Malik from a loving rural boy who accidentally kills a caterpillar, to lich — the end point not of corruption, but of *complete understanding*.

## Core premise

Malik is not a dark lord. He is a naturalist. He does not raise the dead out of hunger for power — he raises them because he cannot bear entropy. Every stage of his life is an attempt to understand death more completely, so he can push back against it more precisely. The lich transformation is not a fall. It is the logical conclusion of a scientist who made himself the experiment.

## Tone

- LitRPG: system notifications, codex entries, skill tiers, mana economy
- Science of magic: the system boxes use period-accurate vocabulary (vital spirits, humoral substrate, pneuma, animating soul) — not modern biology
- Emotionally grounded: Malik is always recognizably the boy who wept over a caterpillar
