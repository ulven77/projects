# Ersättningsfond

**A dedicated savings account that accumulates monthly accruals toward the future purchase cost of a specific asset.**

In the fleet cost model, each vehicle has an Ersättningsfond funded by a fixed monthly deposit (Ersättningsreserv). The deposit is calculated as (expected replacement cost − expected residual value) ÷ months until replacement. Unlike consumer depreciation (Avskrivning), which tracks falling market value, Ersättningsfond tracks rising accumulated savings — making it visible whether you are on track to fund the next car before the current one needs replacing. First encountered in the vehicle financial reality report, 2026-05-14.
